## 🛠️ How to Follow a Roadmap
1. 💡 **Survey the Landscape:** Look at the entire roadmap to understand the scope.
2. 🎯 **Set a Goal:** Pick a specific milestone (e.g., "Learn React Hooks").
3. 🏗️ **Build Something:** Apply what you learn immediately by building a small project.
4. 📚 **Consult Multiple Sources:** Use MDN, YouTube, and official documentation.
5. ⭐ **Review Regularly:** Technology evolves; keep your skills up to date.
